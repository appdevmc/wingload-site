Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Wing Load Calculator respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store or transmit any personal information.

Information Stored

The application stores a small amount of information locally on your device to improve your experience. These settings never leave your device.

  • Preferred language
  • Selected theme (Light / Dark / System)
  • Measurement units (Metric / Imperial)
  • Last entered calculation values

Personal Data

Wing Load Calculator does not collect, process or transmit:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Camera access
  • Microphone access
  • Analytics data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage tracking

Internet Connection

The application works fully offline for all core functionality. No Internet connection is required to calculate Wing Loading.

Third-Party Services

Wing Load Calculator does not include advertising, analytics, tracking or any third-party services that collect user information.

Children's Privacy

Wing Load Calculator does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any future changes will be published on this page.
