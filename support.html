Frequently Asked Questions

How is Wing Loading calculated?

Wing Loading is calculated by dividing the jumper's exit weight by the canopy size.

Does the application work offline?

Yes. Wing Load Calculator works completely offline and does not require an Internet connection.

Does the application collect personal data?

No. The application does not collect, transmit or store any personal information.

Can I choose my canopy based only on this application?

No. Always consult a qualified instructor before changing canopy size.
