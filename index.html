About Wing Loading

Wing Loading is one of the most important factors affecting parachute performance. It influences forward speed, landing characteristics, flare power and overall canopy responsiveness.

Wing Load Calculator is intended for educational and planning purposes only. Always consult a qualified instructor before changing canopy size.
